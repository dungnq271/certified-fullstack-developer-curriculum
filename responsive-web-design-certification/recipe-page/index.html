Tortilla De Patatas

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making Tortilla De Patatas, one of the most popular dishes in Spain. This classic and simple dish is perfect for satisfying your appetite. Follow this simple recipe to create Spanish's national omelette dish that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 4 (350g/12.3 oz) medium potatoes
  • 5 eggs (size L)
  • 250 ml (1 cup) olive oil
  • salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Add the oil to a skillet heat until smoking. Then, add the potatoes to the skillet and lower the temperature to medium. Poach the potatoes, often stirring, so the potatoes do not stick together or to the bottom. When done, you should have tender potatoes that are not fried.
  2. Transfer the potatoes to a colander, let them drain a bit, and cool.
  3. Add the eggs to a large bowl and mix them with a fork to break them. There is no need to incorporate air and whisk excessively. Season with salt.
  4. Now, add the potatoes to the eggs and season with salt again. Stir and let the mixture sit for a few minutes to blend.
  5. Heat a 23cm/9-inch nonstick skillet until hot. Then, add a bit of olive oil to the skillet and pour the egg and potato mixture into the skillet. Lower the temperature to medium-low and cook for a minute.
  6. Then invert the tortilla onto a plate. Add a bit of olive oil to the skillet and heat over high heat. Slide the tortilla into the skillet so the raw side is on the bottom, and lower the heat to medium-low. Cook for a minute.
  7. Transfer the tortilla to a plate and let it rest for a few minutes (it will continue to cook as it cools!), then cut. If you want the liquid insides, don't let the tortilla rest; cut it immediately once out of the skillet.
